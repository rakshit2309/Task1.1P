WATCH DOG

The role of Watchdog is to enhance disaster monitoring and rescue operations by providing a comprehensive set of features and capabilities. Its primary function is to act as an advanced monitoring system that captures live video feed of the disaster location through the ESP sensor, enabling rescue teams to remotely assess the situation in real-time. This visual information helps them make informed decisions and plan their approach effectively. Additionally, Watchdog incorporates a portable button phone that serves as a communication tool between the rescue team and trapped victims. This feature allows victims to receive instructions, make customized calls, and utilize the phone's torch function when needed, providing them with a lifeline and a means to seek immediate assistance.
